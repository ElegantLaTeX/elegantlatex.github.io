ElegantLaTeX introduction

ElegantLaTeX Program is created by Ethan Deng and Liam Huang, and maintained by Ethan Deng, syvshc and sikouhjw. We are intended to provide some beautiful, elegant, user-friendly templates.

Currently, the ElegantLaTeX is composed of ElegantNote, ElegantBook and ElegantPaper, which designed for typesetting notes, books, and working papers respectively.

Get the templates

The latest version of templates are released at:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/ElegantLaTeX/
  • CTAN: https://ctan.org/pkg/elegantbook
  • Overleaf: https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates?q=elegantbook
  • LaTeXStudio: https://www.latexstudio.net/index/lists/barSearch/text/elegantbook
